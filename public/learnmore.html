Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the verified badge program

How much does verification cost? expand_more
Verification plans start at $49 per year for the Standard plan, $99 per year for the Professional plan, and $199 per year for the Enterprise plan. All plans require annual renewal to maintain the verified status.
Do I need to renew every year? expand_more
Yes, the verified badge requires annual renewal. You'll receive a notification 30 days before your verification expires. You can also enable auto-renewal for uninterrupted verification.
What happens if I don't renew? expand_more
If you don't renew within the 14-day grace period, your verified badge will be permanently removed. You'll need to reapply and pay the verification fee again to regain the badge.
How long does the verification process take? expand_more
The verification process typically takes 2-5 business days. You will receive an email notification once your application has been reviewed and approved.
Can I get a refund if my application is rejected? expand_more
Yes, if your application is rejected, you'll receive a full refund. You'll also receive feedback on why the application was rejected so you can address the issues and reapply.
Can I lose my verified badge? expand_more
Yes, the verified badge can be removed if you violate Certil.ink's terms of service, engage in fraudulent activity, or fail to maintain an active, professional profile. You will be notified before any action is taken.
